\subsection{Detecting No Solution}

A row of the form
\[
\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c}0&0&0&5\end{array}\right]
\]
represents the impossible equation $0=5$.

\begin{examplebox}
For
\[
\begin{cases}
x+y=1,\\
2x+2y=5,
\end{cases}
\]
row reduction gives
\[
\left[\begin{array}{cc|c}
1&1&1\\0&0&3
\end{array}\right].
\]
The second row says $0=3$, so the system has no solution.
\end{examplebox}

\begin{interpretationbox}[Contradiction means incompatibility]
The contradiction is the algebraic record of geometric conditions that cannot be satisfied simultaneously.
\end{interpretationbox}
