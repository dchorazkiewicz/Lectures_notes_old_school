\subsection{Expansion Along Any Row or Column}

Although the recursive definition uses the first row, the same determinant is obtained from every row or column.

\begin{theorembox}[Laplace expansion]
Expansion along row $i$ gives
\[
\boxed{\det A=\sum_{j=1}^{n}a_{ij}C_{ij}}.
\]
Expansion along column $j$ gives
\[
\boxed{\det A=\sum_{i=1}^{n}a_{ij}C_{ij}}.
\]
\end{theorembox}

\begin{interpretationbox}[Freedom of choice]
The determinant is independent of the row or column chosen for expansion. The choice changes the amount of work, not the value.
\end{interpretationbox}
