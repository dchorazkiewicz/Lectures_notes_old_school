\chapter{Matrices}

\begin{corebox}[From vectors to transformations]
The previous chapters developed geometric objects and their coordinate
descriptions. Matrices now provide a compact language for organising several
coordinates or equations at once and, later, for describing linear
transformations. We begin by learning to read their structure before introducing
operations.
\end{corebox}
