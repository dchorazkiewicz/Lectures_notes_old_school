\chapter{Limits and Continuity}

\begin{corebox}[Behavior before procedure]
A limit describes what values are approached. The chapter develops only the elementary techniques needed to reveal that behavior and uses continuity to compare nearby behavior with the value assigned at a point.
\end{corebox}

\begin{summarybox}[Limits and continuity]

\end{summarybox}
