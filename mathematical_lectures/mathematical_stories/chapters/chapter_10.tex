\begin{summarybox}[Limits and continuity]
Read the type of approach first. Use limit laws and the simplest transformation that exposes the behavior. One-sided and infinite limits describe boundaries and asymptotes. Continuity connects nearby behavior with assigned values and, through the intermediate value theorem, can prove that solutions exist even when they cannot be written explicitly.
\end{summarybox}
