\chapter{Euclidean Geometry and Cartesian Coordinates}

\begin{corebox}[Purpose and learning goals]
This chapter builds the bridge from geometric constructions to numerical coordinates. After completing it, the student should be able to:
\begin{itemize}
\item identify the basic Euclidean constructions used later in the course;
\item construct and read a Cartesian coordinate system;
\item translate geometric distances and directions into coordinate calculations.
\end{itemize}
\end{corebox}
