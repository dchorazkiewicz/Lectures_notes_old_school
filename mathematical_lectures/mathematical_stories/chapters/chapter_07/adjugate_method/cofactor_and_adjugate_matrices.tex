\subsection{The Cofactor Matrix and the Adjugate}

For $A=(a_{ij})$, let
\[
C_{ij}=(-1)^{i+j}M_{ij}
\]
be the cofactor of $a_{ij}$. Arrange all cofactors in the matrix
\[
C=\begin{pmatrix}
C_{11}&\cdots&C_{1n}\\
\vdots&\ddots&\vdots\\
C_{n1}&\cdots&C_{nn}
\end{pmatrix}.
\]

\begin{definitionbox}
The adjugate of $A$ is the transpose of the cofactor matrix:
\[
\boxed{\operatorname{adj}(A)=C^{\mathsf T}}.
\]
\end{definitionbox}

\begin{remarkbox}[The transpose is essential]
The adjugate is not the cofactor matrix itself. Rows and columns must be exchanged after all cofactors are computed.
\end{remarkbox}
